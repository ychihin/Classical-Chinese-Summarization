In the first year of the ordinary year, the reign name was changed, and a general amnesty was granted. Civil and military officials were rewarded based on their achievements, and agricultural officials who were filial and loyal were given noble ranks. Extremely poor families were exempted from taxes, and widows, widowers, and orphans received assistance. A solar eclipse occurred. Xiao Hong, King of Linchuan, was appointed Taiwei and governor of Yangzhou, while Xiao Jing was reassigned to Yingzhou. Wang Lai resigned due to his mother's death, and Wang Feng succeeded him. A bright star appeared in the east. Xiao Gang was appointed governor of Yizhou.
