The moon opposed the east phase star. In the third year, the moon covered the stars. Mercury clashed with Regulus. Yu Kai and others mobilized the army; martial law was imposed. Mars moved against the normal path and violated Taiwei Yuan, portending the emperor fighting in the countryside and the prime minister's death. Mars moved against the normal path and was located northwest of the left law enforcement star, portending the law enforcement minister's death. The moon covered Regulus and the Dipper.
