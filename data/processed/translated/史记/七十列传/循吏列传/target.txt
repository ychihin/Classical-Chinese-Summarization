Sun Shuao was a hermit from Chu State.
The Prime Minister Yu Qiu recommended him to King Zhuang of Chu, hoping that he would succeed him.
Sun Shuao was promoted to the state minister in three months after he became an official. He administered politics and taught the people, which made the officials and people harmonious and united, and the customs were very pure and beautiful. His administration was lenient and not harsh, but he always stopped prohibitions. Officials did not do evil or deceitful things, and there were no thieves among the people.
In autumn and winter, he encouraged people to go into the mountains to cut trees, and in spring and summer, he used the rising river water to transport the wood out of the mountains.
The people all have convenient ways to make a living and live happily. King Zhuang believed that the original coins of Chu State were too light, so he ordered the small coins to be minted into large coins. It was very inconvenient for the people to use them, and they gave up their own business one after another.
The chief executive in charge of the market reported to Prime Minister Sun Shuao: The market is in chaos, no one can do business there with peace of mind, and the order is very unstable.
Sun Shuao asked: How long has this been going on?
The city order replied: It has been three months.
Sun Shuao said: Needless to say, I will try to restore the market to its original state now.
Five days later, he went to the court to advise King Zhuang: "The reason why I changed the coins before was because I thought the old coins were too light."
Now the city magistrate has come to report that the market is in chaos, no one can make a living there with peace of mind, and the order is very unstable.
I request an immediate order to restore the old currency.
King Zhuang agreed and issued the order for three days, and the market returned to its original state.
The people of Chu State liked to ride in low carriages. The King of Chu thought that low carriages were not convenient for driving horses, so he wanted to order the low carriages to be raised.
Sun Shuao, the Prime Minister of the State, said: It is not good to issue government orders repeatedly, leaving the people at a loss as to what to do.
If you definitely want to raise the height of your car, I would like to ask the villagers to raise the threshold.
The passengers are all respectable gentlemen. They cannot get out of the car frequently just to pass the threshold, so naturally the base of the car will be made higher.
The king of Chu agreed to his request.
After half a year, people took the initiative to raise the height of their cars.
This is why Sun Shuao naturally obeyed his teachings without giving orders to control the people. People around him saw his words and deeds with their own eyes and imitated him. People who were far away watched the changes in the people around him and imitated him.
Therefore, Sun Shuao was not complacent in being in the position three times, because he understood that he had achieved it with his talents; he had no regrets in leaving the position three times, because he knew that he was not at fault.
Zichan was a senior official in the state of Zheng.
When Zheng Zhaojun was in power, he appointed his favorite Xu Zhi as the prime minister of the country. The government was in chaos, the officials and the people were not in harmony, and the father and son were not harmonious.
Dagong Ziqi told Zheng Zhaojun about these situations, and Zhaojun appointed Zichan as the prime minister.
After Zichan was in power for one year, the libertines no longer played frivolously, the elderly no longer had to carry heavy loads, and the children no longer had to work in the fields.
After two years, transactions in the market will be fair and high prices will no longer be reserved.
Three years later, people did not close their homes at night and did not pick up lost items on the road.
Four years later, farmers will not have to take their farm tools home after work. Five years later, men will not need to perform military service. In case of a funeral, they will consciously perform funeral rites.
Zichan passed away in the twenty-sixth year of his rule in Zheng. The young men cried bitterly, and the old people wept like children, saying: "Zichan has left us and died. Who will the people rely on in the future?"
Gongyixiu was a doctor from the state of Lu.
Because of his outstanding talent and learning, he became the prime minister of the state of Lu.
He obeyed the laws and acted according to the principles without changing the regulations at all, so the officials behaved themselves in a correct manner.
He ordered officials not to compete for interests with the people, and high officials not to take advantage.
A guest brought fish to the Prime Minister Gong Yixiu's door, but he refused to accept it.
The guest said: I heard that you love fish very much, so why don’t you accept it?
Gong Yixiu replied: "It's because I love fish so much that I can't accept it."
Now that I am the Prime Minister, I can afford fish. If I am dismissed from office because I accepted your fish today, who will be willing to give me fish in the future?
So I can't accept it.
Gongyixiu ate the vegetables and thought they tasted good, so he pulled out all the mallow vegetables in his home and threw them away.
When he saw that the cloth he had woven was good, he immediately kicked his wife out of the house and burned the loom. He said: Are we going to leave farmers and weavers with nowhere to sell the goods they produce?
Shi She was the Prime Minister of King Zhao of Chu. He was strong, upright, honest and just. He was neither flattering nor timid.
On a trip to inspect various counties, a murderer happened to kill someone on the way. He pursued the murderer, who turned out to be his own father.
He let his father go and imprisoned himself when he returned.
He sent someone to tell King Zhao: The murderer is my father.
If you punish your father to establish political achievements, this is unfilial; if you abandon the law and condone crime, it is disloyal; therefore, I deserve the death penalty.
King Zhao said: "You chased the criminal but did not catch him. You should not prosecute the crime. You should go and manage the affairs of the country."
Shi She said: If you do not favor your father, you are not a filial son; if you do not abide by the king's law, you are not a loyal minister.
Your forgiveness of my sins is a favor from the Lord; serving your sentence and dying is your duty as a minister.
So Shi She refused to obey the king of Chu's order and cut his neck to death.
Li Li was a judge of Duke Wen of Jin.
He heard that the case was wrong and the murder was in vain. When he found out, he imprisoned himself and sentenced him to death.
Duke Wen said: Official positions vary, and punishments vary in severity.
This is the fault of your officials, not your fault.
Li Li said: My official position is that of a senior official, and I have never given up my high position to my subordinates. I have received a lot of official salary, and I have never given any benefits to them.
Now, I have made mistakes in investigating the case and a life was wasted, but I have to shift the blame to my subordinates. I have never heard of such a theory.
He refused to accept Duke Wen's order.
Duke Wen said: If you think you are guilty, am I guilty too?
Li Li said: There are laws and regulations for judges to decide cases. If you are wrongly sentenced, you will be punished personally, and if you are wrongly killed, you will be punished with death.
You made me a judge because I can listen to subtle details and decide difficult cases.
Now I heard the case wrongly and killed someone in vain, so I should be sentenced to death.
So he refused to accept Duke Wen of Jin's pardon and committed suicide with his sword.
