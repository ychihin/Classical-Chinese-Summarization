Yang Zhenxie was born with a crooked jaw and a crooked forehead, and his ugly appearance frightened people.
His mother wanted to abandon him, but his father disagreed and said, "I heard that many sages have strange appearances."
It is this son who prospered my clan.
So he kept it.
In the first few months, he knew how to be humble; when he went to school at the age of ten, he could understand the "Book of Songs" and "The Analects of Confucius"; when he grew up, he specialized in the "Book of Rites" and "The Book of Changes".
Don't read non-sacred books, don't practice greetings and asking questions.
There was a thatched house built by the predecessors on the hill, with fields below. Zhou Xie often worked here to support himself.
I won’t eat the rice I didn’t grow or the fish I caught. The Xiangdang clan wanted to see him.
Later, he was praised as filial, honest, virtuous and upright. When he was called, he was called sick.

The clan even advised him: People practice morality for the sake of the country.
Since our ancestors, we have been honored and favored continuously. Why do you guard the Pitian of Donggang alone?

So he went to Yangcheng, Yingchuan, sent his disciples to express his gratitude, and then returned home.

His wife searched for him everywhere, but there was no trace of him.
Later, he saw a dead chariot and a dead horse in the grass, and his clothes were rotten. He thought they were killed by tigers, wolves, and thieves, so he mourned them.
It took more than ten years before he returned to his hometown. He was determined and upright, never immoral, and treated his wife as seriously as a monarch and his ministers. The township party followed him as an example.

◆The biography of Shen Tu Pan, whose courtesy name is Zilong, is from Chen Liuwai Huang.
My father died when I was nine, and I grieved excessively.
After taking off his mourning clothes, he stopped eating meat and wine for more than ten years.
On the anniversary of his father's death, he often goes without eating for three days.
A woman named Yu from the same county, named Yu, avenged her father and killed her husband's party. The officials arrested Yu and reported it to Liang Pei, the order of Waihuang. Liang Pei planned to execute Yu.
Shen Tupan was only fifteen years old at the time and was studying. He asked Huang Ling and Liang Pei to advise him: "Jiu Yu's integrity is enough to impress the shameless grandson and inspire the tolerant son."

Liang Pei thought this was a good statement, so he was sentenced to prison.

Shen Tupan's family was very poor and he was employed as a painter.
Guo Linzong was surprised when he saw it.
Cai Yong of the same county valued Pan very much. When he was called up by the state, he gave way and said: "Shentu Pan had a mysterious temperament and a sensitive mind. When his father died, he paid homage to him, which almost ruined himself."
His good conduct is difficult for others to achieve.
Be content with poverty, cultivate your moral character, and not deteriorate due to climate change, nor lose your integrity due to poverty.
Compared with me, Cai Yong, he is older than me and more virtuous than me.
Later, the county called Shen Tu Pan to be the chief bookkeeper, but he didn't.
So he lived in seclusion at home, studied knowledge intensively, mastered the "Five Classics", and also understood the study of diagrams and latitudes.
He began to study at Taixue with Prince Ju of Jiyin. When Zi Ju was about to die, he entrusted himself to Pan, who personally pushed the cart to send the funeral back to his hometown.
When he met Sili, he was working between He and Gong. He thought that Shentu Pan was very loyal, so he passed the talisman to him and sent someone to escort him. Pan refused to accept it, threw the talisman on the ground and left.

Taiwei Huang Qiong summoned him to serve as an official, but he refused to go.
