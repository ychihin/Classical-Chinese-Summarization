The custom of Xiqiang is that clans are not fixed. Some clans use the father's name and the mother's surname as the title of their tribe.
After the twelfth generation, clans intermarry with each other. After the father dies, he marries his stepmother, and after his elder brother dies, he marries his widower. Therefore, there are no widowers or widows in the Xiqiang country, and the population reproduces quickly.
Xiqiang does not have a king or ministers, and no one is its leader. If the power is strong, they will separate into races and become chiefs. If the power is weak, they will become subordinate tribes of others. They plundered and invaded each other, ruling the roost with violence.
