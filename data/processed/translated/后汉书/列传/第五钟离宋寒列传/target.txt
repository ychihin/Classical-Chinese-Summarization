He himself thought that he would not be able to be a high official for a long time, so he moved his family to Hedong, changed his name, and called himself Wang Boqi. When he grew up, he liked the study of Confucian classics, had extensive knowledge of books and biographies, and taught his disciples in "Shang Shu".
He was promoted as Xiaolian.
During the Yongping period, he served as the imperial censor as a visitor, and jointly tried Yan Zhong and Wang Ping in the Chu prison with the three government officials. His confessions implicated the Hou Xiang Hou Geng Jian, Lang Ling Hou Zang Xin, Hu Ze Hou Deng Li, and Qu Cheng Hou Liu Jian.
Liu Jian and others stated in their confessions that they had never met Yan Zhong and Wang Ping.
At this time, Xianzong was furious, and the officials were afraid. Anyone involved would be involved in the case, and no one dared to forgive based on emotion.
The emperor said: Even so, the four princes are fine, why not report it early and let the lawsuit drag on for a long time?
Han Lang replied: Although I checked them and found nothing, I was afraid that others in the country would expose their adultery, so I didn't dare to report them in time. Emperor Fa cursed angrily: "The officials can't make up their mind like this, so hurry up and arrest him."
The people on the left and right were about to arrest Han Lang, but Han Lang said: I am willing to say a word and die.
A small minister does not dare to deceive the king, but only wants to help the country. The emperor asked: Who will discuss with you? He replied: I know that my clan should be exterminated, and I dare not pollute others too much. I only sincerely hope that Your Majesty will wake up.
When I see those who are imprisoned, they all say that they are evil and evil, and I should all hate them. Those who come out now are not as good as those who came in, so they do not need to be held accountable.
Therefore, taking one test will lead to ten, and taking ten tests will lead to hundreds.
Also, when the ministers met in court, when your majesty asked about gains and losses, he always knelt on the ground and spoke. The great crime of the old system had harmed the nine tribes, but your majesty was so kind that he only punished his own sins, and the world was very lucky.
When I returned home, I didn't say anything, but I raised my head and sighed. No one knew how wronged I was, and no one dared to disobey His Majesty.
What I am saying now is indeed that I will die without regrets. The emperor understood what he meant and issued an edict asking Hanlang to go out.
Two days later, the emperor went to Luoyang to interrogate the prisoners and cleared out more than a thousand people.
Later, Ping and Zhong died in prison, so Hanlang imprisoned himself.
In case of amnesty, he will be removed from his official position.
He was also promoted as Xiaolian.
During the early years of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Suzong held a meeting with ministers, and Hanlang came forward to express his gratitude. The edict recognized that Hanlang had given loyal advice to the late emperor, and he was appointed as the county magistrate of Yizhou.
After more than a year, he was promoted to the order of Luoyang. He resigned due to the death of his mother and the people missed him.
In the first year of Zhanghe, the emperor went on a tour to the east and passed through Jiyang. Three old officials wrote a letter describing the political situation before Hanlang.
When the emperor arrived in Liang, he summoned Hanlang and ordered the three prefectures to be appointed chiefs, thus promoting Hanlang to the Situ Prefecture.
During the Yongyuan period, he was promoted to the prefect of Qinghe, but was dismissed for violating the law.
In the third year of Yongchu's reign, Taiwei Zhang Yu recommended Han Lang as a doctor and conquered the public carriage. He met Han Lang and died at the age of eighty-four.
