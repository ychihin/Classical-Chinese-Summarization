Zhang Rang, Duan Wanggui and others could not escape the siege, so they kidnapped the emperor and dozens of King Chen Liu and fled to Xiaopingjin.
The ministers all came out from Pingtang, and no one ran with them. Only Lu Zhi, the minister, rode on horseback on the river at night. Wang Yun sent Minggong from central Henan to follow Lu Zhi.
Mingong arrived and killed several people with his sword, while the rest jumped into the river and died.
The next day, the ministers and officials welcomed the emperor back to the palace, appointed Min Gong as the doctor, and granted the title of Marquis of Ting.
Dong Zhuo then deposed Emperor Ling, forced the Queen Mother to be killed, and killed Lord Wuyang. The He family died, and the Han Dynasty also fell into chaos.
