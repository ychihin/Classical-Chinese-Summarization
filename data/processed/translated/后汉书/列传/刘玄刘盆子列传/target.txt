So he burst into tears and lamented. Fan Chong and other hundreds of people present felt pity for him, so they all left their seats and kowtowed, saying: "I have failed your majesty. From now on, I will not dare to indulge again." So they picked up the basin together and tied a seal ribbon on him.
Liu Penzi called for help.
After the banquet, everyone closed their camps and defended themselves. The three assistants unanimously praised the emperor as wise.
The people rushed back to Chang'an, and the city was full of people.
More than twenty days later, Chimei was greedy for money and went out to plunder again.
When the food in the city ran out, he collected and loaded up treasures, so he set fire to the palace and led his troops westward.
After passing through the southern suburbs to worship the sky, the chariots, armor, soldiers and horses were the most vigorous, with a crowd of millions.
Liu Penzi rode in the king's chariot, driving three horses, followed by hundreds of horsemen.
So he plundered from Zhongnan Mountain to the big city and Xiaoyi, fought with Gengshi General Yan Chun in the county, defeated Yan Chun and killed him, and then entered Anding and Beidi.
When they arrived at Yangcheng and Fanxuzhong, they encountered heavy snow and the pits and valleys were full. Many soldiers froze to death. So they returned, excavated the tombs in the mausoleums, and stole the treasures in the tombs. Empress Lu's body was also insulted. In the tombs excavated by Chimei, most of the jade boxes containing the remains looked like they were alive, so Chimei was able to do a lot of dirty work.
The great Situ Deng Yu was in Chang'an at this time and sent troops to attack Yu Yi. However, he was defeated by Chi Mei, and Yu had to leave Yunyang.
In September, Chimei returned to Chang'an and lived in Guigong.
At this time, the Hanzhong thief Yancen sent troops to disperse the pass and stationed himself in Duling. Pang'an led more than 100,000 people to attack Yancen.
Deng Yu led his army to attack Chang'an because Pang'an's elite troops were away and only Liu Penzi and some weak people lived in the city.
Just then Xie Lu's reinforcements arrived and started a night battle with Deng Yu's army on Cha Street. Deng Yu was defeated and fled.
Yancen and Gengshi general Li Bao combined tens of thousands of troops to fight against Pang'an at Duling.
Yancen and others were defeated and more than ten thousand people died. Li Bao surrendered to Pang'an. Yancen gathered the scattered soldiers and fled. Li Bao sent someone to inform Yancen, saying: "You try your best to fight back. I will turn back from the inside and attack from both inside and outside, which can greatly destroy Pang'an."
Yancen immediately returned to challenge, Pang'an Kongying fought back, and Li Bao pulled out all the red eyebrow army flags from behind and replaced them with his own banners. Pang An and others returned to camp after being exhausted from the battle. When they saw that the flags had been replaced by white flags, they ran in panic and threw themselves into a valley. More than 100,000 people died. Pang An and thousands of others escaped and returned to Chang'an. At this time, there was a great famine in Sanfu, people were eating people, the city walls were empty, and the fields were full of bones. The people who stayed often formed camps to protect each other, and each of them stood firm.
Chimei couldn't grab anything, so in December, he led his troops back eastward, leaving more than 200,000 people scattered along the way.
Guangwu then sent General Hou Jin and others to garrison in Xin'an County, and General Jianwei Geng Yan and others to garrison in Yiyang, dividing the troops into two groups to intercept their return.
He ordered the generals to say: If the thieves go east, they can lead Yiyang's troops to join Xin'an; if the thieves go south, they can lead Xin'an's troops to join Yiyang. In the first month of the second year, Deng Yu crossed from Hebei and attacked Chimei in Hu County. Deng Yu was defeated again, so Chimei left the pass and marched south.
General Feng Yi defeated the Red Eyebrow Army in Guodi.
After Guangwu heard the news, he personally led his army to Yiyang and blocked their escape with a powerful army.
Chimei suddenly encountered a large army and was so shocked that he didn't know what to do, so he sent Liu Gong to beg Guangwu for surrender, saying: "Penzi has surrendered millions of people to your majesty. How will your majesty treat them?" The emperor said: I will just not kill you. Fan Chong then stripped Liu Penzi, Prime Minister Xu Xuan and more than 30 people and asked Guangwu to surrender.
Guangwu received a royal seal, a seven-foot sword from Gengshi, and a jade jade.
The captured weapons were piled in the west of Yiyang City, as high as Xiong'er Mountain.
The emperor ordered the county chef to give food to the Red Eyebrow Army. The soldiers were hungry and sleepy for a long time, and more than 100,000 people were able to have a full meal.
Early the next morning, the emperor arranged his troops and horses beside Luoshui, and ordered Liu Penzi's monarchs and ministers to line up to watch.
The emperor said to Penzi: "Do you know that you deserve to die?"
I will let you go back to the camp now. You can lead the army to attack each other with drums and decide the outcome. I don't want you to force yourself to obey me. Xu Xuan and others kowtowed and said: When we left the east capital gate of Chang'an, the emperor and his ministers discussed and surrendered to the Holy One.
The people can rejoice with them after the event is completed, but it is difficult to plot with him at the beginning, so we did not tell the people in advance.
Returning today is like escaping from a tiger's mouth and returning to a loving mother. I am so happy that I have no regrets.
The emperor said: You are indeed the kind of tough guy, an outstanding talent among mediocre people. He also said: You are extremely unrighteous, killing the old and weak wherever you go, urinating on the country and polluting the wells, water, pots and stoves.
But there are three more advantages: conquering cities all over the world without abandoning the original wives and concubines, this is one advantage; being able to use the Liu family clan when establishing a monarch, this is the second advantage; other thieves established kings, and when they were desperate, they often surrendered with their heads. You thought you had done a good job, but you let Penzi live to surrender to me, this is the third advantage. So they were ordered to live in Luoyang with their wives, and each was given a residential area and two hectares of land.
In the summer of this year, Fan Chong and Pang An rebelled and were executed.
When Yang Yin was in Chang'an, he was kind to Zhao Wang Liu Liang and granted him the title of Marquis of Guannei. Both he and Xu Xuan returned to the countryside and died at home.
Liu Gong killed Xie Lu for Gengshi, tied himself up and went to prison to plead guilty, but was pardoned and refused to kill him.
Emperor Guangwu loved Penzi, rewarded him with many things, and made him the doctor of King Zhao.
Later, his eyes became ill and he became blind, so he was given the official land of Junshu in Xingyang, opened a shop, and allowed him to use the tax revenue here for the rest of his life.
