Yang Guang reported to Dou Xian, so Dou Xian reported that Zheng Hong had leaked secrets as a minister.
The emperor ordered Zheng Hong to take back his seal and ribbon.
Zheng Hong came to Tingwei to plead guilty and ordered him to leave. Because he asked to resign, he was not allowed to do so.
When he was seriously ill, he wrote a letter to express his gratitude and pointed out Dou Xian's shortcomings.
The emperor read the memorial and sent a doctor to see Zheng Hong's illness. By the time the doctor arrived, Zheng Hong had already died.
Before his death, he returned all the things the emperor had given him and ordered his wife and children to be buried in brown turbans and plain coffins before returning to his hometown.
◆Zhou Zhangzhuan, also known as Uncle Zhou Zhang, was a native of Sui County, Nanyang County.
At first, he was a meritorious official in the county.
At this time, the general Dou Xian was granted the title of champion and returned to the country.
Zhou Zhang went from the prefect to the championship, and the prefect also wanted to pay homage to Dou Xian.
Zhou Zhangjin remonstrated: "Today we are traveling for official business, how can we go beyond etiquette and make private friends with powerful people?"
Moreover, Dou Xian is a relative of the concubine and has great influence on the royal family. Now that he has retired to the vassal state, it is difficult to measure the blessings and misfortunes.
You are the Minister of Talisman Dissection, and you have a heavy responsibility of thousands of miles. How can you act rashly when you move forward or retreat?
The prefect refused to listen and wanted to raise the chariot.
Zhou Zhang went to draw his sword and cut the leather around the horse's neck, and the prefect was forced to stop.
When Dou Xian was executed, most of the officials below the public minister were offended for associating with Dou Xian, but the prefect was spared, so they valued Zhou Zhang.
He was promoted to be filial and honest, and he was promoted to the rank of Langjiang with five sense organs in the sixth year.
In the first year of Yanping, he was named Guang Luxun.
In the first year of Yongchu, he succeeded Wei Ba as Taichang.
In the winter of this year, Dai Yinqin was appointed Sikong.
At this time, the ministers Zheng Zhong and Cai Lun were using their power to intervene in the government affairs, and Zhou Zhang spoke out several times.
At first, after the death of Emperor He, Empress Dowager Deng thought that the prince Liu Sheng had a chronic illness and could not flatter the ancestral temple. Emperor Shang was a young child and was raised as her own son, so she made him the prince and made Liu Sheng the king of Pingyuan.
After the death of Emperor Shang, the ministers thought that Liu Sheng's illness was not a chronic illness, and all advocated the appointment of Liu Sheng. Since the Queen Mother had not established Liu Sheng before, fearing that the queen would resent her, she appointed Liu Sheng, the son of Prince Xiao of Qinghe, the emperor's brother, as Emperor An.
Zhou Zhang thought that the public was unwilling to follow him, so he conspired to close the palace gates, kill the brothers of Chariot and Cavalry General Deng Zhi, Zheng Zhong, and Cai Lun, hijack the minister, deposed the Queen Mother in the South Palace, made Emperor An the king of Yuan, and made Liu Sheng, the king of Pingyuan, the emperor.
The plot was discovered, the plan was dismissed, and Zhou Zhang committed suicide.
The family had no money left, so they changed their clothes and went out to eat by the sun.
