Guanzhong soldiers rushed to the Chu-Han battlefield in large numbers, while Peng Yue and Tian Heng occupied the Liang area, attacking and harassing the Chu army and cutting off their food channels.

The king of Han was furious and wanted to attack him.

Xiang Yu knew that he lacked helpers and was about to run out of food and grass. Han Xin led troops to attack the Chu army many times, and he was very worried.
The King of Han sent Duke Hou to Chu to persuade Xiang Yu to release the Grand Duke and Empress Lu.
So Xiang Yu and the King of Han agreed to divide the world equally, using the chasm as the dividing line, with the west returning to Han and the east returning to Chu.

The King of Han also had plans to return west to Guanzhong, but Zhang Liang and Chen Ping dissuaded him, saying: The King of Han now owns most of the world, and princes from all over the country have come to join him. But now the Chu army is exhausted and has run out of ammunition and food. This is really the best opportunity given by God to destroy Chu. If we let go of Chu people at this time, we will be raising tigers and causing trouble.
The king of Han listened to their opinions.
