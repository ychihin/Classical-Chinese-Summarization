Hong Gong, Shi Xian and others knew that Xiao Wangzhi had a long history of noble integrity and refused to give in and be insulted. They suggested: Xiao Wangzhi, a former general who assisted in state affairs, wanted to exclude the Xu and Shi families and control the state affairs exclusively.
Fortunately, he was not punished, and was given a title, a house, and participated in the discussion of national politics. He did not repent of his mistakes and confessed his crimes. He also harbored resentment and instigated his son to write a letter attributing his fault to the emperor. He thought that because of his status as a master, he would not be punished.
If Xiao Wangzhi is not allowed to suffer some humiliation in prison and block his dissatisfaction, then the Holy Dynasty will not be able to bestow favors on him.
The emperor said: Mr. Xiao has always been upright, how can he accept the interrogation of officials?

The emperor approved his report.
Shi Xian and others sealed the emperor's approval and handed it over to the visitor, and ordered it to be delivered to Xiao Wangzhi personally. Then they ordered Taichang to quickly lead the Jinwu cavalry to surround his house.
The messenger arrived and sent word to Xiao Wangzhi.
Xiao Wangzhi wanted to commit suicide, but his wife stopped him, thinking that this was not the emperor's will.
Xiao Wangzhi asked his disciple Zhu Yun about this.
Zhu Yun was a man who loved fame and honor, so he advised Xiao Wangzhi to commit suicide.
At that time, Xiao Wangzhi looked up to the sky and sighed and said: I once served as a general, and I am over sixty years old. Isn't it too despicable to go to prison at an old age and live an ignoble life? The factory called Zhu Yun and said: You, come and get the medicine. I would rather die than stay alive for a long time!
Finally he committed suicide by drinking poisonous wine.
The emperor was shocked when he heard about this. He clapped his hands and sighed and said: I suspected that he refused to go to jail before, but he really killed my good teacher!
At that time, the eunuch had just served lunch when the emperor pushed away the food and cried for Xiao Wangzhi. His sorrow moved the attendants around him.
Immediately summoned Shi Xian and others to question them for their poor planning.
They all took off their hats to apologize, and it took a long time before they gave up.
Xiao Wangzhi died guilty, and the relevant departments requested that his title and title be abolished.
However, the emperor issued an edict to increase his favor, and his eldest son Xiao Ji succeeded him as Marquis of Guannei.
The emperor could not forget Xiao Wangzhi's memory, and sent envoys to pay homage to Xiao Wangzhi's grave during every year and festival. This was the case throughout the Yuan Dynasty.
Xiao Wangzhi had eight sons, among whom Xiao Yu, Xiao Xian and Xiao You became high officials.
Xiao Yu was named Cijun. When he was young, he was appointed as the prince's concubine due to his father's kindness.
Emperor Yuan ascended the throne and served as a Langguan. He was dismissed due to illness and later became a censor.
General Wang Feng, because of Xiao Yu's father's reputation and his own outstanding talents, appointed him as a meritorious officer, promoted to Yezhi, and served as deputy captain on an envoy to the Huns.
Later, when he became the magistrate of Maoling County, during an inspection, Xiao Yu ranked sixth.
However, Guo Shun, the magistrate of Qixian County, ranked last and was scolded. Xiao Yu interceded with him. Fufeng got angry and said, "You came sixth in the exam and you got away. How can you intercede for your colleagues?"
After the examination, he went out and summoned Maoling to Hou Cao to ask him to report on the situation during his tenure.
Xiao Yu walked straight out of Hou Cao, and the clerk followed him and grabbed him. Xiao Yu held down his saber and said: Xiao Yu is Du Ling's man, why should he go to Hou Cao!
He hurriedly walked out, wanting to abandon his official position and leave.
The next morning, the emperor issued an edict to summon him and appointed him as Sili Xiaowei.
Xiao Yu passed by the gate of Fufeng Mansion, and officials and hundreds of people gathered in front of the car to pay their respects.
Later he was dismissed from office for violating the general's will.
He also served as Zhonglang General on an envoy to the Huns.
He successively served as the governor of Jizhou and Qingzhou counties, the captain of Changshui, and the prefect of Taishan. He went to Beijing to serve as Dahonglu.
Because Liang Zizheng, a famous thief in E County, took advantage of the mountains to harm one party and was unable to capture him for a long time, Xiao Yu Ren You Fu Feng Fu Yue later killed Liang Zizheng and his gang.
Later, he was dismissed from office because he was particularly close to Chunyu Chang, Marquis of Dingling.
During the reign of Emperor Ai, there were many thieves on the Yangtze River in Nanjun, so Xiao Yu was granted the title of Grand Administrator of Nanjun.
Because Xiao Yu was a famous official, the emperor used the car used by the three princes to carry Xiao Yu into the palace to accept the order. He said: "There are groups of thieves in Nanjun who are harming the people. I am very worried about this matter."
Because the prefect has always had a high prestige, he was appointed as the prefect of Nanjun. After taking office, he should only eliminate harm and maintain safety for the people. Don't get stuck in small details.
Reward twenty pounds of gold.
Xiao Yu went to Nanjun and the thieves were eliminated.
He was dismissed from office due to illness, and was appointed as Guanglu doctor to hold Jinwu, and he lived in his position for the rest of his life.
Xiao Yu was a dignified and brave man. He was dismissed several times as an official and was rarely promoted.
When he was a child, he was friends with Chen Xian and Zhu Bo, and he was very famous at that time.
In the past, Wang Yang and Gong Gong were good friends, so there is a saying in Chang'an: Xiao Yu and Zhu Bo tied the knot and became good friends, and Wang Yang and Gong Gong got to know each other after playing their crowns. It means that they recommended each other and became famous.
At the beginning, both Xiao Yu and Chen Xian were famous for being the sons of Gongqing. Chen Xian was the first to enter the palace. He was appointed Zuocao at the age of eighteen and Yushi Zhongcheng in his twenties.
At that time, Zhu Bo was still the head of Duling Pavilion. He was introduced by Chen Xian and Xiao Yu and entered the Wang family.
Later, he served as governor, county governor, and state minister at the same time, and later he served as Jiuqing. Zhu Bo first served as general and minister, and experienced more official positions than Chen Xian and Xiao Yu, all the way to prime minister.
Xiao Yu and Zhu Bo later had a quarrel and could not end well, so the world thought it was difficult to make friends.
Xiao Xian lived there and became prime minister. He was promoted to Maocai and appointed magistrate of Liaoqi County. He was promoted to Jinbao, Hui Neishi, Zhang Zheng, Wang Di, and Jiongsai prefect.
Wherever he went, he made political achievements and received many increases in salary and gold rewards.
Later, he was dismissed from the official position and served as the captain of the Yue Cavalry School, the captain of the guard army, and the general of Zhonglang. He was sent as an envoy to the Huns and reached the rank of Da Sinong. He died in his post.
Xiao You, whose courtesy name was Zijiao, was appointed as the Prime Minister's general of Xicao Wei. He was promoted to the rank of Yezhi and was appointed as the deputy captain of the envoy to the Xiongnu.
Later, he was recommended as a virtuous person and appointed as magistrate of Dingtao County. He was promoted to captain of Taiyuan and prefect of Anding.
He is prestigious in governing states and counties, and many people praise and recommend him.
When Emperor Ai became King of Dingtao, Xiao You was appointed as the Dingtao Order, which went against the wishes of King Dingtao. Soon, the court dismissed Xiao You as a commoner.
After the death of Emperor Ai, he was appointed Captain of Futu, Captain of Jingfu and Zuofu, and was promoted to prefect of Jiangxia.
Because of their meritorious service in wiping out the bandits on the Yangtze River, Cheng Zhong and others were given an increased salary and were appointed as the prefect of Chenliu. During the first year of the Yuan Dynasty, Mingtang and Piyong were built, princes and kings were convened for large-scale court meetings, and Xiao You was recruited to serve as Dahonglu. It happened that he was ill and could not host etiquette and receive guests. He returned to his original official position, but was dismissed due to illness.
Later, he served as a doctor in Zhongsan and died in office.
