Literati must use their intelligence skillfully and reveal their ambitions differently. Therefore, they are prone to look down on their peers and show off their arrogance.
On big occasions, he will insult and despise princes and kings; on small occasions, he will look down on and insult his friends, which will lead to jealousy and sin.
For example, Qu Yuan and Mai Yi were ostracized and exiled, and Huan Tan and Feng Yan were reprimanded and dismissed. Sending them out was something that only happened once in a generation. It was really the disaster brought about by the arrogance of these literati!
The literary men recorded above were in an era of prosperous education, and they wrote articles with gorgeous rhetoric. They did not have depression and worries, and they did not encounter the disasters that the previous generations of literati suffered. It is really great.
The discussion shows that he is a kind of person who pays attention to destiny.
Sages rarely talk about things like fate. They tend to talk about fate and believe that fate must control life. This is not the meaning of the sages' classics.
