Later, Xianyu Xiuli was killed by Yuan Hongye, and his men were scattered. Ge Rong, the general of Huaishuo Town, took the opportunity to gather Xianyu Xiuli's scattered soldiers, attacked and killed Tujinluozhou, and annexed all Tujinluozhou's troops. People called him "Ge Thief".
In the fourth year of Xiaochang, Emperor Wei Ming died, and Empress Dowager Hu came to rule. Tianzhu General Er Zhurong raised troops from Jinyang and entered Luoyang, killing Empress Hu and Hu's relatives.
Hou Jing initially brought his army to see Er Zhurong. Er Zhurong thought that Hou Jing had a genius and immediately appointed him as a general to lead the troops.
When they were dispatched, Ge Rong's bandits marched south to Luoyang. Er Zhurong went to conquer it in person. He ordered Hou Jing to be the vanguard and led his army to Hanoi to attack Ge Rong. He defeated Jiang's army and captured Ge Rong alive. Because of his great achievements, Hou Jing was promoted to the governor of Dingzhou, Daxingtai, and was granted the title of Duke of Puyang.
Hou Jing became famous from then on.
Soon, Emperor Shenwu of Qi became the prime minister of Wei. He led his army into Luoyang and killed the Zhu family. Hou Jing led his army to surrender to Gao Huan and was appointed by Emperor Shenwu.
Hou Jing was cruel and tyrannical, and controlled the army strictly. However, all the treasures he plundered from the enemy were distributed to the soldiers, so the soldiers were happy to serve him and often won battles.
